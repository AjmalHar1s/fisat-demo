IMPULSE 4.0

IMPULSE 4.0 IMPULSE 4.0, the fourth version of the flagship event of the IEEE Student Branch of FISAT will be hosted as a three day National Event at Federal Institute of Science and Technology,Ernakulam, Kerala on the dates 13th, 14th and 15th March 2020. The Techno- Managerial event will include technical workshop sessions and invited speakers sessions along with a small cultural program and icebreaking sessions.

IMPULSE, the flagship event of the IEEE Student Branch of FISAT has a legacy of four years of exuberating volunteering and active participation from engineering colleges across the country. Impulse was introduced as a one day National Event by the pioneers of FISAT IEEE SB, 2015 Execom, and happily accepted by the successors and now being conducted as three day events since 2016. The fourth version of the event is upcoming and the students are enthusiastically awaiting for yet another amazing experience.

ABOUT

FISAT

Federal Institute of Science and technology(FISAT) is a post graduate self financing engineering college, established on 2002 and run by the Federal Bank Officers Association Educational Society(FBOAES). FISAT is situated in Hormis Nagar at Mookannoor, near Angamaly in Ernakulam District, Kerala. The college has a builtup area of over 7 lakh sq.feet which consist of academic area , labs, seminar halls, conference halls and Auditorium. The college is accredited by NAAC with A grade. The college campus provides state of art equipments with Sun Blade servers and campus wide WiFi, with a speed of 240 MBps. The IEEE FISAT SB of FISAT is going on string with a membership of more than 200 every year. A successful SB, it holds two societies(IEEE RAS and IEEE CS), and has been among the most active SB in kerala
